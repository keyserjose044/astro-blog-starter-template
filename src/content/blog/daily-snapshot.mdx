## üîß 4. Behind the Scenes ‚Äî INDEX, IFERROR, and Drive Integration

<figure>
  <img src="/images/behind-the-scenes-summarygs.png" alt="Behind the scenes of the Summary GS sheet showing INDEX and IFERROR formulas" />
  <figcaption><em>Behind the scenes: 52 formulas bring every piece of data together into a single snapshot.</em></figcaption>

Underneath the visual layout lies a network of formulas ‚Äî primarily **INDEX()**, **IFERROR()**, and **MATCH()** ‚Äî all working in harmony.  
Each section references its own sheet:  
`People`, `Places`, `Pics`, `RD1`, `RD2`, and more.  

Even the **Photo of the Day** is automated: Google Drive links are parsed with a REGEX formula and displayed using the `IMAGE()` function, creating a seamless Photo-A-Day experience directly in Sheets.
